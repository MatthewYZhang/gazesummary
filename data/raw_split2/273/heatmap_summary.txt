Social media has transformed the social, political, and economic landscape, evolving from earlier forms of online communication like forums and chat rooms to platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. This evolution is driven by technological advancements, societal trends, and entrepreneurial innovation. The emergence of Web 2.0, with user-generated content and interactive applications, allowed users to create profiles, connect, and share updates, departing from passive information consumption. Social media's future will be shaped by technologies like AI, AR, and blockchain to refine personalized experiences, create immersive environments, and enhance data privacy and security. This evolution is ongoing, influenced by technological innovation, societal needs, and human interaction. Understanding these factors is vital for navigating social media's challenges and opportunities.
